Privacy Policy

Respect for your choices and information boundaries

Overview

ColorBox does not collect personally identifiable information. Data is processed locally on your device, and exports occur only when you explicitly choose to share.

How We Handle Data

  • Local storage: preferences, palettes, and recent items are kept on your device.
  • Image processing: color picking and analysis happen locally; nothing is uploaded.
  • Export & sharing: performed only when you explicitly choose to export or share.

Permissions

  • Files/Photos: used to select images for color picking; no scanning or uploading of other files.
  • Clipboard: used to copy color values and configurations; access is limited to user actions.

Third‑Party Libraries

We use open‑source components for color computation and interface features. These components do not include logic to collect personal information.

Your Choices

  • Clear local data anytime: settings include options to clear cache and reset.
  • Transparent exports: all exports are user‑initiated, visible, and controllable.

Contact Us

For questions or suggestions, please open an issue in the repository. We’ll respond thoughtfully.
